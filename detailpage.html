Medical History

Rocket has minor points on his teeth, so we have reduced pellets from his diet to encourage great hay consumption. He is maintaining a good weight on no pellets, so he will not require teeth trims unless he begins showing clinical signs of issues (ei. drooling, weepy eye). Neutered 7/14/22.

Diet

Pellets: 1/2 Oxbow Essentials Adult pellets (AM)
Hay: Unlimited TIMOTHY hay only (all day)
Salad: 1 cup romaine and cilantro (PM)
Snack: 2-3 times a week

Story

Rocket wasn’t always the confident bunny he is today. When he was first rescued, he was timid and hesitant to explore the world around him. With a bit of love and patience, Rocket’s true personality began to shine, revealing a curious, adventurous spirit. Now, Rocket is the star of his foster home, hopping from room to room like the intrepid explorer he is. He’s fascinated by everything new—whether it’s a cozy corner to snuggle into or a new toy to nudge around. Despite his adventurous streak, Rocket has a gentle heart. He loves a good nose rub and will sit quietly by your side, enjoying peaceful moments. Rocket’s ideal forever home is one where he has space to explore and someone who appreciates his mix of curiosity and calmness. Whether it’s helping you relax after a long day or amusing you with his playful hops, Rocket is ready to be your little ray of sunshine.
