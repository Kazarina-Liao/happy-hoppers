Welcome to Happy Hoppers

What We Do?

We’re a nonprofit organization dedicated to connecting bunnies in need with loving families. Our mission is to provide a safe and nurturing environment for rabbits while they wait for their forever homes. We provide medical care to individual bunnies and focus on their wellbeing, ensuring they’re ready to thrive in a new home. Through compassionate adoption services, education, and ongoing support, we help both bunnies and their new families build lasting, happy relationships.
